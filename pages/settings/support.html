Frequently Asked Questions
How do I connect my Instagram account? +
Go to Add Platform in the sidebar, then click Connect Instagram. You'll be redirected to Instagram to authorize DMFlow. Make sure your account is a Creator or Business account.
Why is my automation not triggering? +
Check that: (1) Your automation is set to Live, not Paused. (2) The keyword someone commented matches exactly. (3) Your Instagram account is still connected and authorized.
What is the DM limit on Free plan? +
Free plan allows up to 500 DMs per month. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited DMs and advanced features like Multi-Link and Lead Capture.
How do I cancel or pause an automation? +
Go to Automations → My Automations and toggle the switch next to any automation to pause or resume it. You can also delete it from the same screen.
